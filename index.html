What this is, and why it matters

Your campaign website is the front door to your candidacy. It's where voters land after a Google search, where they end up after a text message, and increasingly where AI assistants pull facts about who you are. Getting it right starts with getting the foundational information right — and that's what this intake is for.

We don't want to guess at your priorities. We want to understand them deeply enough to build a site, write the copy, design the brand, and structure the AI-readable data so that your narrative — not your opponent's, not Wikipedia's, not a half-remembered news clip — is the one that voters and search engines find.

Everything you tell us in the next steps becomes the source material for the work ATP and Mirror Factory do on your behalf. Be honest, be specific, and don't worry about polishing the language — we'll handle the polish.

What we'll ask about

Sixteen short steps, organized around the building blocks of your campaign.

01

Identity & race

Who you are, what office you're seeking, jurisdiction, party, election dates.

02

Your story

Bio, why you're running, what makes your candidacy different, the messages that drive you.

03

Issues & positions

The policy areas you care most about and where you stand on each.

04

Brand & visuals

Logo, headshot, colors, photos. Don't have them yet? We can help.

05

Operations

Committee details, fundraising platform, donation page, compliance specifics.

06

Voter outreach

SMS/MMS preferences, surveys, social channels, the consult call cadence.

What happens after you submit

01

You finish the intake

Your answers are saved securely and assets you upload go to a private ATP folder for our team.

02

ATP reviews

We confirm timeline, scope, and any open questions. You'll hear from us within 24 hours.

03

We build

Mirror Factory uses your intake to generate a custom 7-page campaign site, AI-optimized for visibility.

04

You review & launch

You see a staging link, send back consolidated feedback, we apply edits, and we launch.
