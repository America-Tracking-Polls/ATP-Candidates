COMPANY BIO

The official narrative of America Tracking Polls. Use this copy as the foundation for all marketing materials, press releases, and partner communications.

America Tracking Polls (ATP) is a Florida-based election research and campaign services firm headquartered in Ocoee, FL 34761. Founded on January 1, 2025, ATP was built on a single conviction: campaigns that listen to voters win elections. While most political firms treat polling and marketing as separate disciplines, ATP fuses them into one continuous feedback loop that gives candidates an unfair advantage at the ballot box.

ATP provides voter engagement guidance, compliance resources, and data-driven best practices for local, statewide, and federal races across the United States. We don't just collect data—we turn raw voter intelligence into actionable, multi-channel campaign strategies that reach voters where they are and move them to action.

Intelligence + Engagement + Persistence + Conversion = Victory.

This is our formula. We poll your voters to learn what they actually care about. Then we use those insights to power every piece of your campaign—from the way your website talks about issues, to how AI search engines describe you, to the direct mail piece that lands in a voter's mailbox with a QR code linking to a personalized survey.

Our services span the full campaign lifecycle. We start with Political Polling—rigorous, statistically valid research that reveals where you stand, what voters want, and where your opponent is vulnerable. From there, we deploy a suite of distribution tools: AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) ensures AI platforms and search engines accurately represent your positions. VRM (Voter Relationship Management) organizes your voter data into a system that tracks engagement, sentiment, and conversion across every touchpoint. Campaign Sites are built to convert—not just inform. SMS Outreach delivers your message directly into voters' hands, fully TCPA/10DLC compliant. Digital Ads target persuadable voters with data-backed messaging. And Direct Mail with QR-coded print bridges the physical and digital worlds, driving offline voters into your online ecosystem.

ATP stands apart because we believe in 7 Proven Strategies that win elections—a methodology refined across real campaigns, not academic theory. We deliver a 95% Voter Reach rate by combining channels that reinforce each other rather than operating in silos.

PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGERY

All photography in the ATP brand follows a strict treatment: black & white base with a blue gradient overlay sweeping from one side. Never use full-color photography.

Treatment Examples

B&W + Gradient Overlay
B&W + Gradient Overlay
B&W + Gradient Overlay

Do

  • Convert all photos to grayscale using CSS filter: grayscale(100%)
  • Apply a gradient overlay: red to blue at 30% opacity using ::after pseudo-element
  • Feature images of voters, polling locations, campaigns, American landmarks
  • Maintain high contrast in the base B&W image
  • Use images at 600px minimum width for cards, 1200px+ for heroes

Do Not

  • Use full-color photography anywhere in the brand
  • Use stock photos with watermarks or generic corporate imagery
  • Apply other color treatments (sepia, duotone with off-brand colors)
  • Use low-resolution or pixelated source images
  • Feature partisan symbols (donkey/elephant) in branded materials

ANIMATION GUIDELINES

Motion in the ATP brand is subtle, data-driven, and purposeful. It reinforces the polling intelligence theme without distracting from content.

Hero Canvas Animation

Use canvas-based data visualizations showing competing red (#d42b2b) and blue (#3a5fd9) polling lines oscillating around a 50% midpoint. Gradient fills underneath each line at 15% opacity. Pulsing live dots at the right edge.

Scroll Reveals (GSAP)

Use GSAP + ScrollTrigger for all scroll-based animations. Default: fade up with y: 30, opacity: 0 to y: 0, opacity: 1. Stagger siblings at 0.15s. Easing: power2.out. Trigger once at top 70% viewport.

Transition Philosophy

Motion should feel like data appearing—measured and confident. Prefer scroll-reveal (fade up, stagger) over flashy transitions. No spinning, flipping, bouncing, or elastic effects. Data-driven, not playful.

TONE OF VOICE

ATP's voice is data-driven, authoritative, and direct. Every piece of copy should reinforce one thing: we help candidates WIN through polling intelligence and multi-channel distribution.

Data-Driven

Lead with numbers, stats, and evidence. Never make vague claims. Instead of "We help campaigns," say "We deliver 95% voter reach across 7 proven channels."

Authoritative

Speak with confidence. Use declarative sentences. "Our formula works." Not "We think our approach might help." ATP knows what wins elections.

Direct

Short paragraphs. Active voice. No filler words. Every sentence should either inform or persuade. If it does neither, cut it.

Write Like This

  • "We poll your voters, learn what moves them, and put that intelligence to work across every channel."
  • "7 proven strategies. 95% voter reach. One goal: victory."
  • "Real insights. Real distribution. Real results."
  • "Data doesn't lie. Neither do we."

Not Like This

  • "We're a full-service agency that helps campaigns achieve their goals."
  • "Our innovative solutions leverage synergies to drive engagement."
  • "We're passionate about making a difference in politics."
  • "Contact us to learn more about our exciting offerings!"
